# Career Aspirations & Strengths

She is interested in roles such as:

- AI Solutions Architect

## Key Strengths

### Systems-Level Thinking
Her clearest strength is the ability to hold an entire system in her head simultaneously — from hardware constraints at the bottom to application behavior at the top. In carrier-grade telecom, where a firmware bug means truck rolls for thousands of customers, this systems-level thinking is critical.

### Code Quality & Engineering Discipline
She writes code meant to be reused, debugged, and maintained by others. Documentation, meaningful naming, explicit error handling, and thorough code review are non-negotiable practices. She has followed CMMI Level 3 quality processes throughout her career.

### Fast Learner
Every major skill beyond her formal education — Yocto, U-Boot, YOLO model training, Android development, FastAPI, Docker, Kubernetes — was self-taught through determination and hands-on practice. She consistently proves she can learn new technologies quickly and deliver results.

### Real-Time Systems Expertise
Deep understanding of RTOS constraints: priority-based scheduling, interrupt latency, mutex/semaphore management, deadlock avoidance, shared memory synchronization. This background shapes her approach to all software design — she thinks in terms of determinism, resource contention, and failure modes.

### Multi-Platform Deployment
She has demonstrated the ability to deploy the same ML model across three very different environments: mobile (Android/TFLite), edge device with GPU (Jetson Nano/TensorRT), and cloud/server (FastAPI/ONNX/Docker/Kubernetes). This is the complete skillset of an ML deployment engineer.

### Communication & Documentation
Years of coordinating offshore teams, writing functional specifications, and authoring design documents have made documentation and clarity a reflex. She writes technical blog posts to share her experience with the community.

The common thread: moving from one complex technical domain to another, each time proving she can master new skills rapidly while maintaining engineering excellence.
